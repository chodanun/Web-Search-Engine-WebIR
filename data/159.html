Activities

Taipei, Taiwan (3-5 October 2012)


Institute for Information Industry invited Dr. Kongkiti Phusavat to give a keynote speech in the conference (Open the Windows of New Electronic Commerce in Southeast Asia). His speech focused on infrastructure readiness, support and promotion by the public sector, and consumer behavior. The conference was held in Taipei, Taiwan.





Celje, Slovenia (20-22 June 2012)

          
Dr.Pornthep Anussornnitisarn was invited by International School for Social and Business Studies (Slovenia) and the city of Celje (Chamber of Commerce) to join the business-academic panel in the promotion of Small and Medium Enterprises. Dr. Pornthep focused on the roles of information and communication technology, logistics, and productivity as key success factors in business development. In addition, Dr. Kongkiti Phusavat and Dr.Punnamee Sachakamol joined Dr. Pornthep in participating in the conference organized ISSBS which is called MakeLearn 2012 in the capacity of Editorial Panelist and Conference Board Member.


 

      

      

      

     

Lublin, Poland (22-25 May 2012)

Dr.Pornthep Anussornnitisarn and Dr. Kongkiti Phusavat participated in the Editor Panels organized by Maria Curie-Sklodowska University (Lublin, Poland) for Technology, Innovation, and Industrial Management (TIIM) 2012 Conference. TIIM has been organized since 2009 and has been held in Bangkok (Thailand), Pattaya (Thailand), Oulu (Finland), and Lublin (Poland) respectively.
